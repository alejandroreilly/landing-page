Where Are Common Foxes Found?

Arctic foxes are found in Arctic tundra habitats of North America, Eurasia, and Fennoscandia.

Fennec foxes are found in the sandy, arid regions of North Africa.

Red foxes are found in North America, Asia, Europe, and North Africa.

Silver foxes are found in North America, Russia, and Australia.
